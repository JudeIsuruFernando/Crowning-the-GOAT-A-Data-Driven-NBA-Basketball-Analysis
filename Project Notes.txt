Project Introduction
Glossary:-

draft_year - The year the player was picked

draft_round - The round in which the player was picked. There are 2 rounds. Round 1 picks are considered more promising. Pre-1989 draft year, there are more than 2 draft rounds.

gp - Games Played - How many games that season

pts - Points - Average points scored per game

reb - Rebounds - Average times they grabbed a missed shot per game

ast - Assists - Average times they passed to someone who scored per game

net_rating - Net Rating - When this player is on court, does the team outscore opponents or get outscored? Positive = good

oreb_pct - Offensive Rebound % - Out of all possible offensive rebounds while he was on court, what % did he grab?

dreb_pct - Defensive Rebound % - Same idea but for defensive rebounds

usg_pct - Usage % - How much of the team's plays "go through" this player — high % = ball is in his hands a lot

ts_pct - True Shooting % - How efficiently does he score? Accounts for 2-pointers, 3-pointers AND free throws

ast_pct - Assist % - Out of all teammate baskets while he was on court, what % did he set up?

PG - Point Guard

SG - Shooting Guard

SF - Small Forward

PF  - Power Forward

C - Center
